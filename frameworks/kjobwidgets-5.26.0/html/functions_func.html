About

Widgets for tracking KJob instances

Maintainer
The KDE Community
Supported platforms
Linux, MacOSX, Windows
Community
IRC: #kde-devel on Freenode
Mailing list: kde-frameworks-devel
Use with CMake
find_package(KF5JobWidgets)
target_link_libraries(yourapp KF5::JobWidgets)
Use with QMake
