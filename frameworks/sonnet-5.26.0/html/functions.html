Support for spellchecking

Maintainer
Martin Tobias Holmedahl Sandsmark
Supported platforms
Linux, MacOSX, Windows
Community
IRC: #kde-devel on Freenode
Mailing list: kde-frameworks-devel

target_link_libraries(yourapp KF5::SonnetCore KF5::SonnetUi)
