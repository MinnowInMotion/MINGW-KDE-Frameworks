About

Support for bookmarks and the XBEL format

Maintainer
The KDE Community
Supported platforms
Linux, MacOSX, Windows
Community
IRC: #kde-devel on Freenode
Mailing list: kde-frameworks-devel

find_package(KF5Bookmarks)
target_link_libraries(yourapp KF5::Bookmarks)
Use with QMake
QT += KBookmarks
