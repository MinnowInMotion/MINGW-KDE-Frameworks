Detailed Description

abstract class for KNotification actions

A KNotificationPlugin is responsible of notification presentation. You can subclass it to have your own presentation of a notification.

You should reimplement the KNotificationPlugin::notify method to display the notification.

Author
Olivier Goffart <ogoffart at kde.org>
